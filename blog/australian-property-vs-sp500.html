50 Years of Australian Property vs S&P 500

Published: July 2025 · Tepuy Solutions Research

Australian investors have long debated between putting their money into real estate or the share market. But what does the data actually show over the long run? We’ve compiled five decades of statistics and transformed them into clear visuals so you can make more informed decisions.

Conclusion

While property remains a cornerstone for many Australians, data suggests that diversified exposure to global equities—particularly the S&P 500—can offer superior long-term returns. The choice depends on your goals, risk profile, and timeframe.
