Why Property?

Property is traditionally seen as a stable investment that provides both long-term capital appreciation and potential rental income. However, it requires high upfront costs, ongoing maintenance, and is less liquid compared to shares.

Why Shares?

Investing in shares offers flexibility, liquidity, and the chance to diversify across industries. The potential for high returns is balanced by volatility and market risk. Shares also make it easier to scale in and out of investments over time.
